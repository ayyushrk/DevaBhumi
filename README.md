# 🛕 DevaBhumi
### *Echoes of the Eternal*

---

DevaBhumi is an open digital sanctuary dedicated to the sacred temples of India. From the misty shores of Kerala to the ancient corridors of Tamil Nadu and the spiritual highlands of Karnataka — every temple has a story. DevaBhumi brings those stories to life.

---

## Features

- 🔍 **Search & Explore** — Find temples by name, region, deity, or architectural style
- 🏛️ **Temple Profiles** — Rich history, significance, architecture and mythology

- 📅 **Timings & Festivals** — Pooja schedules and major festival dates
- 🌐 **Regional Coverage** — Kerala · Karnataka · Tamil Nadu · Pan India

---

## Temple Coverage

| Region | Notable Temples |
|--------|----------------|
| 🌴 Kerala | Padmanabhaswamy, Guruvayur, Sabarimala, Vadakkunnathan |
| 🏔️ Karnataka | Udupi Sri Krishna, Kukke Subramanya, Dharmasthala |
| 🌺 Tamil Nadu | Meenakshi Amman, Brihadeeswarar, Ramanathaswamy |
| 🇮🇳 Pan India | Tirupati, Kashi Vishwanath, Somnath, Kedarnath |
